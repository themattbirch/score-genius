Quick Start Guide

ScoreGenius provides real-time NBA analytics, predictive insights, and betting recommendations. Here's how to get started:

  • Install the app from your browser or device's app store
  • View today's NBA games and predictions
  • Track live game progress with real-time score predictions
  • Explore betting insights and recommendations

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are predictions updated?

Predictions update in real-time during games. Quarter-by-quarter models improve accuracy as games progress.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our models achieve increasing accuracy throughout games, with Q4 predictions reaching ~4.7 RMSE (significantly better than early-game predictions).

Does the app work offline?

Yes, previously loaded data and predictions are cached for offline viewing, though real-time updates require an internet connection.

Troubleshooting

Predictions Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the app
  • Clear app cache and try again

Chart Display Issues

  • Ensure your app is updated to the latest version
  • Try switching between data visualization modes
  • Restart the app
