About ScoreGenius

I’m Matt Birch, the developer and lifelong sports fan behind ScoreGenius. I live and work in Virginia, where I combine my background as a sportswriter with over a decade of web-development experience to build tools that bring clarity to the games we love.

Built by Matt Birch

My Approach

I launched ScoreGenius to give fellow fans transparent, data-driven insights without the noise. Every prediction, chart, and snapshot is powered by an API-first pipeline I architected myself—from Supabase materialized views to the PWA you’re using now.

I believe software should start with people, not abstract requirements. Before writing a line of code, I ask: “What question is the fan really trying to answer?” That focus drives everything—from a lightweight, dark-mode UI to stadium-level weather forecasts and injury reports that load instantly. I favor clean, scalable architecture so the app evolves as leagues, data sources, and your feedback do.

Behind the Scenes

On any given day you’ll find me refining a prediction model for MLB, NBA, or NFL; tweaking our service worker caching; or iterating on Joyride tours to make the app intuitive.

Outside of code, I’m often on local hiking trails or perfecting my pour-over coffee—little rituals that keep me curious, energized, and ready to solve the next puzzle.
