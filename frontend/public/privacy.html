Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2025

Our Commitment

ScoreGenius does not collect or share any personal or sensitive user data. We only use data that you explicitly provide or that is publicly available for NBA analytics and predictions.

Data We Use

  • Preferences: favorite teams, theme (light/dark), notification settings
  • Prediction History: past forecasts stored locally for your reference
  • Cached Game Data: downloaded NBA stats for offline use
  • Anonymous Usage Data: we do not collect any analytics or performance metrics

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties for any purpose, including advertising or marketing.

Permissions

ScoreGenius requests only the minimal permissions needed:

  • Local Storage: save your preferences and cached game data
  • Network Access: fetch the latest NBA stats and predictions

Your Choices

You can clear all stored data at any time by uninstalling the PWA or clearing your browser’s storage for scoregenius.io.
